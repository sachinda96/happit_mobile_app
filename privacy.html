Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 8, 2026

Happit ("the App", "we", "us") is developed and operated by an independent developer ("the Developer"). This Privacy Policy explains what information Happit collects, how it is used, and the choices you have. By using Happit, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

If you have questions, you can contact us at: sachindanipunkumara@gmail.com

1. Information We Collect

Account information
When you create an account or sign in, we collect:

  • Email/password sign-up: the email address and name you provide.
  • Sign in with Google: your name, email address, and profile photo, as authorized by you through Google.
  • Sign in with Apple: your name and email address (or Apple's private relay email if you choose to hide your email), as authorized by you through Apple.

We use Firebase Authentication (a Google service) to manage sign-in and store your account credentials securely. We never see or store your password in plain text — Google's Firebase Authentication handles this.

Habit and app data
To provide the core functionality of the app, we store the content you create, including:

  • Habit names, icons, colors, and frequency (e.g., daily, weekdays, weekends).
  • Your completion history for each habit (the dates you mark a habit as done).
  • Optional reminder times you set for a habit.

This data is stored in Google Cloud Firestore, tied to your account, and is only accessible to you. It is used solely to display your habits, calculate streaks and statistics, and power reminders inside the app.

Notifications
If you choose to enable reminders for a habit, Happit requests permission to send local notifications on your device. These reminders are scheduled entirely on your device — we do not use push notification services, and no notification content is sent to or stored on our servers. You can disable notification permissions at any time in your device settings.

Information we do not collect
Happit does not collect or access your:

  • Location
  • Contacts
  • Camera or photos (other than the profile photo optionally provided by Google/Apple sign-in)
  • Precise device identifiers for advertising purposes

We do not show ads and do not use third-party advertising or tracking SDKs.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information described above only to:

  • Create and maintain your account.
  • Sync your habits and progress across app sessions.
  • Send local reminders you've opted into.
  • Provide customer support if you contact us.

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not share it with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

3. Third-Party Services

Happit relies on the following third-party service providers to operate:

Service Purpose Provider's Privacy Policy
Firebase Authentication Account creation and sign-in firebase.google.com/support/privacy
Cloud Firestore Storing your habits and profile data firebase.google.com/support/privacy
Sign in with Google Optional social sign-in policies.google.com/privacy
Sign in with Apple Optional social sign-in (iOS) apple.com/legal/privacy

These providers process data on our behalf and are contractually/technically restricted from using it for their own purposes beyond operating their service.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on Google Cloud infrastructure via Firebase. Access to your habit data is restricted by security rules so that only your authenticated account can read or write your own data. While we take reasonable steps to protect your information, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your account and habit data for as long as your account remains active. If you would like your account and all associated data permanently deleted, contact us at sachindanipunkumara@gmail.com and we will process your request within a reasonable time.

6. Children's Privacy

Happit is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or the relevant age of digital consent in your country), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

7. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, export, or delete your personal data (for example, under GDPR in the EU/UK or CCPA in California). To exercise any of these rights, contact us at sachindanipunkumara@gmail.com.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of Happit after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us:
